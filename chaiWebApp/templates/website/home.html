What is Chai?

Chai is a popular Indian tea made by brewing black tea with a mixture of aromatic spices and herbs. The word "chai" means "tea" in Hindi, and the drink has a long cultural history across India and beyond. Whether you like it strong or mild, spiced or plain, chai has a flavor for everyone!

Popular Chai Varieties

Masala Chai

A spiced chai made with a blend of traditional Indian spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and ginger. It's rich, flavorful, and perfect for any time of day.

Ginger Chai

A zesty twist on chai made with fresh ginger. It adds a warm, spicy kick and is excellent for boosting your immune system.

Elaichi Chai (Cardamom)

This aromatic chai is infused with cardamom, a spice known for its sweet and peppery flavor, giving chai a refreshing and unique taste.

Benefits of Drinking Chai

  • Rich in antioxidants that can boost overall health.
  • Can aid digestion due to the use of spices like ginger and cinnamon.
  • Provides a natural energy boost from black tea.
  • May help reduce inflammation.
